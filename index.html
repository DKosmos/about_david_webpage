David Kosmos was born and raised in Seattle, Washington but has been lucky enough to travel the world. Business and leisure travel has taken David from the glaciers of Alaska, across the plains of Tanzania, to busling metropolises like Hong Kong, New York, London and D.C.. David has been shaped by these experiences into a man with a global perspective, cultural respect and an enduring curiosity.
